G.Y.A.N.

Guiding Youth with Artificial Network

What is GYAN?
GYAN is an AI-powered chatbot for B.K. Birla Public School’s Creativity Conclave. It helps students and visitors with event details, school information, rules, timings, venues, and more.

How can students from other schools benefit?
Students from other schools can use GYAN to quickly get answers about event participation, schedules, locations, and guidelines, making their conclave experience smooth and informed.
